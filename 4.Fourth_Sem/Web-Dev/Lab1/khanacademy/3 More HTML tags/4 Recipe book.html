Lasagna

  • Time: 1 hour 25 mins
  • Serves: 8 people
Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flour 200 grams
durum wheat semolina flour 100 grams
eggs 3
all-purpose flour 150 grams
butter 150 grams
milk 2 liters
Salt Pepper Nutmeg 1
Grated Parmigiano Reggiano cheese 1
ragù 1 kg

Step 1: Combine the flours, sift them, and create a well in the center. Break the eggs into the well and gently whisk them, gradually incorporating all the flour.

Step 2:Once the flour is incorporated, knead the dough manually for about 10 minutes until a smooth and uniform ball forms.

Step 3: Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest in the refrigerator for about 1 hour.

Step 4: Divide the dough into small portions and pass them through a pasta machine, starting from the thickest setting and gradually reducing it until reaching the desired thickness of 1 millimeter.

Step 5: Cut the rolled dough into lasagna sheets.

Step 6: Boil the pasta sheets in salted boiling water for 2-3 minutes.

Step 7: Submerge the pasta sheets in cold water immediately after boiling to stop the cooking process.

Step 8:Lay the lasagna sheets on a clean kitchen towel to dry.

Step 9: In a saucepan, melt the butter over low heat.

Step 10:Gradually add the sifted flour, stirring vigorously to avoid lumps. Then, slowly pour in the warm milk while continuously stirring to blend the butter and flour mixture.

Step 11:Once the béchamel reaches the desired thick and creamy consistency, turn off the heat and let it rest for a few minutes. Then add salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste.

Step 12: To assemble the lasagna, create 4-5 layers, progressively adding pasta, béchamel, ragù, and grated Parmigiano Reggiano.

Step 13: Bake the lasagna in a preheated oven at 350°F (180°C) for 30-35 minutes.

Step 14:Once a golden crust forms on the surface, remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for 15-20 minutes before serving.

Pici All’Aglione

  • Time: 50 mins
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pici pasta 400 grams
Aglione 35 grams
ithe wine 150 ml
tomato purée 400 grams
diced tomatoes 200 grams
Thyme 1
Chili pepper 1

Step 1: Peel and crush the Aglione, then place it in a pan with olive oil.

Step 2: Sauté the crushed Aglione in olive oil for a few minutes until fragrant.

Step 3: Pour in the white wine and let it evaporate completely.

Step 4: Add the tomato purée and diced tomatoes to the pan.

Step 5: Add chili pepper, salt, and thyme (or basil), and let the sauce cook slowly for about 40 minutes.

Step 6:Add the cooked Pici to the pan and stir them well into the sauce.

Step 7:Serve the Pici with plenty of the rich tomato and Aglione sauce.

Homemade Tagliatelle

  • Time: 4 mins
  • Serves: 8 people
Ingredients Quantity
type 00 flour 350 grams
durum wheat semolina 150 grams
eggs 5
A pinch of salt 1

Step 1: Crack the eggs open and lightly beat them

Step 2: Pour the eggs gradually into the flour previously mixed and salted, placed in a fountain with a central groove (note: salt is not essential) and use a whisk to incorporate all the flour

Step 3: Knead with your hands for about 10 minutes until a smooth dough is formed

Step 4: Cover the dough with plastic wrap and let it rest in the refrigerator for 1 hour

Step 5: Divide the dough into several pieces and pass through a pasta machine, adjusting the thickness settings, until you create strips that are 1 mm thick

Step 6: Run the strips through the lasagna cutter attachment and lay them out on a wide tray, sprinkling some semolina to prevent sticking

Step 7:To cook for 3-4 minutes in boiling water and season the noodles as desired, the noodles are perfect for various condiments, from ragouts to mushroom sauces to those based on fresh vegetables or seafood.
